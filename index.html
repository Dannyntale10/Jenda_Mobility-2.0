Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How long does it take to build a website?

Typical projects take 2-4 weeks from start to launch, depending on complexity. Simple websites can be completed in 7-10 days, while e-commerce or custom applications may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! All our plans include initial support (30-90 days depending on package). After that, we offer flexible maintenance packages starting at $99/month including updates, backups, security monitoring, and technical support.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we build are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. With 60%+ of web traffic coming from mobile, we ensure your site looks perfect on every screen size.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

Yes! SEO and digital marketing are core services. We offer comprehensive SEO packages, Google Ads management, social media marketing, and content strategy to help you reach your target audience and grow your business online.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

No problem! We offer flexible update packages and can train you to make simple changes yourself. For larger updates, we provide hourly rates or can create a custom package based on your needs.

Do I own the website after it's built?

Yes! Once the project is completed and paid for, you own all rights to the website, including the design, content, and code. We'll provide you with all necessary files and documentation.
